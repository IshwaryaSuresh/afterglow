What it is

Share how your day felt as light, not words.

"How was your day?" can be a trap, when naming a feeling is hard, or you're too tired to perform fluency. Afterglow lets you answer in a glow. Your person sees it, and you feel held.

heavy calm content joyful tender

How it works

A six-state ritual, once a day.

Reflect

Set how today felt, in three ways: drag the glow, tap a mood, or type a word. Whichever suits you.

Send

A quiet release. It travels to your person, no read-receipts, no pressure.

Receive

Their glow fills your screen, always with a word, so it's never an anxious guessing game.

Send warmth

No paragraph required. Hold to send warmth back, and they know you sat with them.

Together

When you've both checked in, your two glows meet and breathe as one.

Our story

Your shared days, kept like letters. Re-readable, never a streak to keep up with.
