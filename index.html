The ritual

Three breaths, once a day. That's the whole app.

Afterglow is deliberately small. No feed to scroll, no streak to protect, no number going up. Just one gentle exchange between two people who want to stay close.

1 · Reflect

Pick the feeling that held your day — heavy, calm, content, joyful, tender. Add a word or a note if you like.

2 · Send

Your glow travels to your person. They're told gently — never nagged.

3 · Sit with theirs

When their glow arrives, hold it for a moment to send warmth back. They'll know you stayed a while.

Your story

Every evening, a little more light.

Each day you both show up becomes a ring of light — your two moods blended into one color. Open any day to read it again, exactly as it was: the glows, the words, the warmth that passed between you.

Made for humans

Soft on the outside. Strict where it matters.

  • Your private note is never shared. Not with your person, not with anyone — enforced in the database itself, not just the interface.
  • No streaks, no scores, no guilt. Miss a day and nothing punishes you. Afterglow waits quietly.
  • No ads, no trackers, no data sales. You are the customer, not the product.
  • Made for every kind of mind. Gentle motion, calm pacing, and full support for screen readers and keyboards.
  • Leave whole. Unpair or delete everything, any time, from inside the app.
